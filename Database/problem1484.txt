Now, the fight is won by the team which has higher total combined power. In case of same power, the employees win. You need to check if the employees can win or not.\n\xc2\xa0\nInput\nThe input contains 4 lines: P1, P2, P3 and W, in this order only.\n\xc2\xa0\nOutput\nOutput a single word: "YES" if the employees can win, "NO" otherwise. Don\'t print the quotes in the output.\n\xc2\xa0\nConstraints\n\n1 \xe2\x89\xa4 P1,P2,P3 \xe2\x89\xa4 5\n1 \xe2\x89\xa4 W \xe2\x89\xa4 20\n\n\xc2\xa0\nExample\nInput:\n1\n2\n3\n7\nOutput:\nNO\n\n\xc2\xa0\nExplanation\nWe take 1 + 2 , 2 + 3 or 1 + 3, but all are less than 7.
